Burritos

Burritos are a Mexican food dish where you have beans and any ingredients of your choice

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Refried Beans
  • Tomato
  • Lettuce
  • cheese

Steps

  1. Take the refried beans and put them on an open tortilla
  2. Put the cheese lettuce and tomatos on the refried beans
  3. close the tortilla onto the beans as seen in photo
