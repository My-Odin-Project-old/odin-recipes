Fajitas

Fajitas are a Mexican food dish where you cook steak or chicken with cooked vegetables

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Chicken
  • Avacado
  • Green and Yellow Peppers
  • Small chiles

Steps

  1. Cook the Steak and Chicken
  2. Put vegetables in the pan when meat is about half done
  3. Stir until meat is fully cooked
  4. Put the cooked meat into a folded tortilla
