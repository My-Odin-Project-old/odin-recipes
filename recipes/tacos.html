Tacos

Tacos are a Mexican food dish where you put meat in a tortilla that taste delicous.

Ingredients

  • Tortilla
  • Ground Beef
  • Taco seasoning
  • cheese
  • toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef
  2. Put taco seasoning in when meat is brown
  3. Stir unitl taco seasoning is fully incorpated
  4. Put the cooked meat into a folded tortilla
  5. put cheese ontop of the meat in the tortilla
  6. Add toppings of your choice
